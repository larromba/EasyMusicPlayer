==== Name ====

Easy Music Player

==== Description ====

"Easy Music Player" is a simple and alternative way to play music saved to your phone. This app has been lovingly crafted with only one purpose in mind - to play your music well.

Contains:
- Play, Pause, Next, Previous, Shuffle
- Time scrubber bar
- Repeat none / one / all
- Share music on Twitter & Facebook
- Play music in the background
- Control music from your homescreen and lockscreen

Compatible with iPad and iPhone. Optimised for iPhone 6.
